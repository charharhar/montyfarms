our process

Monty Farms products are made using only the finest, ripest produce cultivated from our farm.

procurement

It all starts at the seed - Monty Farms is proud to procure the best seeds from the world and prime land allocation.

best practices

At Monty Farms we emphasize sustainable farming techniques that preserve soil stability while yielding the best crops by using crop rotation and minimizing chemical use.

best quality standards

Our commitment to adhering to strict standards is unwavering and we utilize third party independent audits of our farms on a regular basis to ensure our farms are running in at the highest quality to produce a consistent supply of our finest fruits and vegetables.

delivery

After harvesting, sorting, storing and packaging, we ensure that only the freshest produce are delivered directly to your door.
