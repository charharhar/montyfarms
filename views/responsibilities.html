Responsibilities

We are committed to improving the lives of our employees and the local community while preserving the environment for many generations to come.

Community

Our objective is to train better citizens and professionals through social assistance and education programs that contribute to a better tomorrow in the communities we operate in.

We are strong supporters of the local community. Over the majority of the past decade, we have worked with a Father at a local church to provide food, medical checkups, education for more than 140 children.

Employees

We prioritize the rights and safety of our employees and provide them with gainful employment free of discrimination and not subject to unfair labor practices.

Environment / Water

At Monty Farms we believe sustainable agriculture is our moral responsibility. We have both national licenses and international certifications that ensure safe disposal of waste, reduced emissions and best machinery maintenance, quality assurance and health and safey practices.
