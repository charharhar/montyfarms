Responsibilities

We are committed to improving the lives of our employees and the local community while preserving the environment for many generations to come.

ENVIRONMENT /WATER / FOOD WASTE

At Monty Farms we believe sustainable agriculture is our moral responsibility. We have both national licenses and international certifications that ensure safe disposal of waste, reduced emissions and best machinery maintenance, quality assurance and health and safety practices. At the heart of our sustainability efforts is a desire to create better tomorrows.

Monty aims to optimize the applied water footprint to crop as well as responsibly manage runoff risks of pollution or contamination of ground or surface water with pesticides, nutrients, or soil. We work with our farmers to develop adequate water management plans for addressing water risk.

In recognition of increasingly scarce natural resources, leftover or unwanted materials have come to be valuable assets rather than waste. We do our best to minimize food waste and find new uses for our produce that do not grow to the appropriate specifications required. We process what we can to flour and any products left we sell to both U.S. Commodities, which distribute to livestock operators, and to local farmers to be used for animal feed.

Community

Our objective is to train better citizens and professionals through social assistance and education programs that contribute to a better tomorrow in the communities we operate in.

We are strong supporters of the local community. Over the majority of the past decade, we have worked with a Father at a local church to provide food, medical checkups, education for more than 140 children.

Employees

We prioritize the rights and safety of our employees and provide them with gainful employment free of discrimination and not subject to unfair labor practices.
